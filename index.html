About

I came from Sitiawan, which is a lively town in Perak, Malaysia, known for its diverse culture and economic activities. It has a rich history with a significant Chinese Foochow community. The town's economy is driven by agriculture, especially oil palm and rubber, along with various local businesses. Popular attractions include the beautiful Tua Pek Kong Temple and the famous Kg. Koh chili sauce. Sitiawan offers a unique mix of cultural heritage and modern commerce, making it a fascinating place to visit.
